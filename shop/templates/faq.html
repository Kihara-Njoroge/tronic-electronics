General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Tronic is only required if you will be selling or buying themes. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I submit my own Bootstrap templates or themes?

A lot of the content of the site has been submitted by the community. Whether it is a commercial element/template/theme or a free one, you are encouraged to contribute. All credits are published along with the resources.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for items, including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD
Sellers

Who cen sell items?

Any registed vendor, who presents products, which is genuine and appealing, can post it on Tronic.

I want to sell my items - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the products section and upload your items
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 24 hours.

How much do I get from each sale?

Here, at Tronic, we offer a great, 75% rate for each seller, regardless of any restrictions, such as volume, date of entry, etc.

Why sell my items here?

There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • A great 75% flat rate for your items.
  • Fast response/approval times. Many sites take weeks to process products. And if it gets rejected, there is another iteration. We have aliminated this, and made the process very fast. It only takes up to 24 hours for products to get reviewed.
  • We are not an exclusive marketplace. This means that you can sell your items on Tronic, as well as on any other marketplate, and thus increase your earning potential.

What are the payment options?

The best way to transfer funds is via Paypal. This secure platform ensures timely payments and a secure environment.

When do I get paid?

Our standard payment plan provides for monthly payments. At the end of each month, all accumulated funds are transfered to your account. The minimum amount of your balance should be at least 70 USD.
Buyers

I want to buy a product - what are the steps?

Buying a product on Tronic is really simple. Once you have selected a product, which is to your liking, you can quickly and securely pay via Paypal.
Once the transaction is complete, we deliver the product to your doorstep.
